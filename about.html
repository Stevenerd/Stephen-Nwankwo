About Me

Red is the freedom road is equivalent to what is happening in the world at large. How the powerless are oppressed because they are weak and have no one to speak for them. The text gave a quick communication of how tables could turn rapidly in life. How a poor person can become rich, how a suffering person can become liberate and how the weak can become strong. Akanji could have maybe survived at the end of the play but his mother's curse must come to pass.

We must say, we learnt from the text that child must not for whatever reason lay hands on his/her parents for the repercussion may be a very terrible one, especially ones mother, a mother is a person that one should treasure and consider the most important in the world. Also the play showed how women are being treated like trash and not taken seriously in the world.
